Welcome to BENCH-PRESS Excerises

Rules:

  1. Bench Press lifter must lie on his back with head.

  2. shoulders and buttocks in contact with the bench surface.

  3. The feet must be flat on the floor (as flat as the shape of the shoe will allow).

  4. His hands and fingers must grip the bar positioned in the rack stands with a thumbs around grip.

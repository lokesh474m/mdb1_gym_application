Welcome to FLOOR-PRESS Excerises

Rules:

  1. Loop the chains over the end of the barbell, and lie down on the floor with your shoulders pulled together and core muscles engaged.

  2. Lower the bar towards the lower chest while tucking the elbows.

  3. Continue to lower the barbell till the elbows touch the floor.

  4. Press the weight back up, and repeat the exercise.

Push-Up

The push-up is a common calisthenics exercise beginning from the prone position. By raising and lowering the body using the arms, push-ups exercise the pectoral muscles, triceps, and anterior deltoids

RUNNING

Running is a method of terrestrial locomotion allowing humans and other animals to move rapidly on foot. Running is a type of gait characterized by an aerial phase in which all feet are above the ground.

DUMBBELL-BRIDGE

The dumbbell bridge is a compound weight training exercise in which the individual weight or resistance away from them using their hands.

BENCH-PRESS

The bench press, or chest press is a weight training exercise where a person presses a weight upwards while lying horizontally on a weight training bench.

PUSH-DOWN

A push-down is a strength training exercise used for strengthening the triceps muscles in the back of the arm. This exercise can also be called a tricep push-down or a two armed standing tricep extension.

CALISTHENICS

Calisthenics or callisthenics is a form of strength training that utilizes an individual's body weight as resistance to perform multi-joint, compound movements with little or no equipment.
